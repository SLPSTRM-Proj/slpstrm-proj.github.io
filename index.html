Our Mission

To make the best fins on the planet — and build a community around the sports that deserve them.

Who we are

We're athletes first. We know what it feels like when your gear holds you back — when the technology simply hasn't caught up with the sport. SlipStream exists to close that gap.

We've started in underwater rugby, but the flow doesn't stem there. Anywhere fins matter, we want to matter.

Why now

Finsports have been flying under the radar for too long. The equipment market has never had a brand that truly understood the sport from the inside.

We're not trying to be the biggest — we're trying to be the best. The kind of brand that people in the sport are proud to be part of.

What we're building

The foundation of high-performance finsports.

Product

Fins engineered specifically for maximum propulsion, pinpoint control, and lasting durability.

Community

Building a dedicated home and culture for competitive finsport athletes worldwide.

Knowledge

Iterative research and development driven purely by athletes in the water, not assumptions.
